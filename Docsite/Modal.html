Modal

Modal component is usefull to display any card or page of less size than the main page in a pop up fashion.

Side Modal

Side Modal is easy to use in your webpage, on a click of a button you can see the modal popping up on the right side and the main page getting blurred. Clicking on the 'x' icon shall close the modal.

You can customise the position of the modal and you can also create an horizonal or square modal like an alert box.
