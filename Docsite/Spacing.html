Spacing

We have some padding and margin properties as utility classes. So you can use them in your project without much hassle.

Padding

We have 6 different sizes of padding and margin that can be applied to top, bottom, right, left or on all sides.

Padding on all sides

To apply padding on all the sides use the following class p-0.
You can change the number from 1 to 6 to alter the size of the padding as per your preference.

Padding on x-axis

We have 6 different sizes of padding and they can be applied to x-axis that'll be applied to left and right side.
To apply padding on left and right both the sides use the following class px-0.
You can change the number from 1 to 6 to alter the size of the padding as per your preference.
